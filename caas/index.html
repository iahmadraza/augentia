Types of Campagin

Over 65% marketers surveyed in year 2016 have confirmed demand generation as their most important priority. Campaigns for demand generation to strengthen your pipeline and to move them beyond MQL

Campaigns to successfully launching your product or service is a key element of your GTM strategy. Product launch campaigns help in introducing the product to your audience and help signup first few clients

Social is an important channel in your marketing efforts, and running consistent campaigns help creating and engaging your community in Facebook or LinkedIn or YouTube. Plan one.

Driving audience to your events or webinars, require careful planning and campaign execution. Creating the right message, and a compelling reason for them to attend is key in such campaigns.

MQL to SQL is an important journey by in itself. This stage in your buyer journey requires neutral content and a consistent touch to remain in their radar. When the micro-moment arrives you better be in front.

Periodic newsletters help in keeping your subscribers engaged. Whether you want monthly or quarterly newsletter, with content feed, we can set it up for you. And also send newsletters and track the consumption too.

Buy one and get one – sales promotions are rampant in B2B space. Whether you run a customer focused promo or a channel promo we are well poised to conceptualize and execute it for you.

Surveys are often seen as a good way to engage your customers and prospects. State your objective and we will create the questionnaires, configure them in polling tools, and drive substantial insights.

Net Promoter Scores (NPS) or a detailed customer feedback enhances your business prospects. Consistently performing satisfaction campaigns and tracking NSAT index helps in focusing on the most important metric.

Your internal audience are as important as your customers. Communicating with them on a regular basis can be institutionalized through internal campaigns and we can run it for you.
